================================================================================
FUTURE SCALE: HOW TO CONVERT TO MICRO-FRONTENDS (MFE)
================================================================================
Currently, this architecture is a "Modular Monolith". It is perfectly organized by 
business domain, guaranteeing high development speed today while ensuring it 
is 100% prepared for Micro-Frontend splitting based on performance needs tomorrow.

When the application scales and performance demands Micro-Frontends:

1. Move the `app/` folder into a new `apps/shell/` container (Webpack Module Federation).
2. Extract the Customer and Business domains (e.g., `business/rider`, `business/marketplace`) 
   into their own standalone repositories or workspaces (e.g., `apps/rider-mfe/`).
3. Extract `core/`, `shared/`, and `infra/` into a shared Monorepo library 
   (`packages/shared-ui`) so all independent Micro-Frontends can consume them.
4. The Shell dynamically imports the distinct Frontend Micro-Frontends at runtime.
